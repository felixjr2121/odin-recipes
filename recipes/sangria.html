Sangria

Description

This authentic sangria recipe is a terrific version of the popular wine drink. You can add any fruit that you want, but I find that apples and pears absorb all the rum. This one is not diluted with carbonated beverages. I have much success with red Burgundy wine and white rum, though spiced run is nice too.

Ingredients

  • 1 orange
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 lime
  • 1.5 cups rum
  • .5 cup white sugar
  • 1 750 ml bottle dry red wine
  • 1 cup orange juice

Directions

  1. Have orange, lemon, lime, rum, wine, and orange juice well chilled.
  2. Slice orange, lemon, and lime into thin rounds; place in a large glass pitcher. Pour in rum and 1/2 cup sugar. Chill in the refrigerator until flavors blend, about 2 hours.
  3. Crush fruit lightly with a wooden spoon; stir in wine and orange juice. Adjust sweetness as desired.
