Espresso Martini

Description

If you love coffee I urge you to try any coffee lover's dream cocktail...Espresso Martini! Techinically it's not a true martini classic French Martini because it doesn't contain any gine or vermouth but it's served in traditional martini glasses and has become just as iconic.

It's strong but sweet, rich, smooth, and utterly delicious it's pretty much like your after-dinner coffee, liquer, and dessert all in one. If an Irish Coffee is your thing you're guranteed to love this! The best part is how quick and easy it is to make, all you need is four easy ingredienta and a cocktail shaker.

If you want to impress at your next dinner party serve this alongside your dessert to really blow your guests away or serve it at the start of the night to really kick things off!
